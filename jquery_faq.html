10 FAQs about Maximum Parks:

Where can I park?
there will be a parking zone near the park that you can park at.
can I hike?
You can hike, as long as you follow the trail provided.
Do I need to bring my Wallet?
You do not need to bring your wallet.
When do you open?
We open at 9am so that people can enjoy the early morning air if they wish to hike
What time do you close?
We close at 5:30pm so employees have time to clean the area and get home.
Can we bring food and drinks?
Sadly you cannot bring food, because of the littering problem sustained from visitors.
Can we feed the animals?
Yes you can, we have feeding stations for the animals with their designated food.
Can we take pictures with the Animals?
You may take pictures with the animals with the guide of one of our employees.
Do you provide Wifi?
We do provide public wifi for the customers to use if need be.
Are there restrooms?
Yes we have plenty of restrooms for costumers to use and changing areas for your babies.
